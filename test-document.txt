Banking System Requirements

The system must provide a secure online banking platform that allows customers to:

1. Create and manage multiple bank accounts with different account types (checking, savings, investment)
2. Perform financial transactions including:
   - Deposit funds into accounts
   - Withdraw cash and transfer between accounts
   - Pay bills and transfer money to external accounts
   - Schedule recurring payments
3. View comprehensive transaction history and account statements
4. Generate customizable reports for tax purposes
5. Set up account alerts for unusual activities
6. Access 24/7 customer support

All transactions must be secure, encrypted, and verified with two-factor authentication. The system must comply with banking regulations and maintain audit trails for all activities.
